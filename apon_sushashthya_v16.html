How do I measure fundal height correctly?
10:44 AM
Ask the woman to lie flat on her back with knees slightly bent. Place the zero end of a tape measure at the pubic symphysis. Measure to the top of the uterus (fundus) in a straight line. In weeks 20–36, the fundal height in cm should roughly equal the gestational age in weeks. A lag of 2+ cm may indicate growth restriction — refer for review.
10:44 AM
What diet should I recommend for a pregnant woman?
10:45 AM
Key advice for pregnant women: Iron-rich foods — green leafy vegetables, lentils, fish; pair with vitamin C (lemon, tomato) to improve absorption. Calcium — milk, small fish with bones, sesame seeds. Protein — eggs, lentils, chicken, fish daily. Folate — dark greens, lentils. Avoid raw fish, excess salt, and highly processed foods. Encourage 8–10 glasses of water daily.
